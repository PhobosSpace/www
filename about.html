Space is the place to be.

Space is the only way the human race can survive. Earth will not last indefinitely, but space will. We need to colonize the universe as fast as physically and humanly possible. The universe is infinite - overpopulation is not possible, and humanity would have no single point of failure. If you don't believe in space, you don't believe in humanity.

We have a mission. One that matters.

Our mission is to assist with the colonization of Mars and the universe, help commercialize human spaceflight, and make space easily-accessible to everyone.

It's bigger than politics & religion

Imagine yourself floating in space looking down at Earth. You see all of these petty politicians complaining and fighting each other. In Space, we must come together as one, to advance the human race.
