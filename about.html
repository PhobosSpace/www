Phobos believes in an intergalactic future

Space is the only way the human race can survive. Earth will not last indefinitely, but space will. We need to colonize the universe as fast as physically and humanly possible. The universe is infinite - overpopulation is not possible, and humanity would have no single point of failure. If you don't believe in space, you don't believe in humanity.

We have a mission. One that matters.

Our current mission is to assist with the colonization of Mars & the universe, help commercialize human spaceflight, & make space easily-accessible to everyone.

Space is naturally exhilarating

Kids, when thinking of rockets and space exploration, typically get excited automatically, as they should. Just imagine floating in the void space. Space is everything.
