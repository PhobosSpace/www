Phobos is back, this time for good

Published on March 1st, 2022

We're Phobos Space and we are making our return, and we are here to stay. Phobos is working on developing advanced technologies to possibly help with the future colonization of Mars, human spaceflight, and spacecraft re-usability. As you probably noticed, we have just released our brand new website with information about Phobos. You may have also noticed that Phobos, originally called Phobos Aerospace, is now shortened to "Phobos Space" and is usually referred to as just "Phobos". We dropped the "aero" because our goal has nothing to do with aircraft, only spacecraft. Phobos is currently not hiring, but we may be in the near future, so be sure to stay tuned.
