Starship Vehicle

SpaceX's Starship spacecraft is a fully reusable space transportation system that is designed to support humans as well as cargo, to Earth orbit, our Moon, Mars, Jupiter, and beyond. The Starship vehicle will be the most powerful launch vehicle ever developed in the world. It will be able to carry more than 150 tons to orbit and back. The long term goal of Starship is to colonize the beautiful planet Mars.
